Frequently Asked Questions

How do I scan multiple pages?

1. Open the app and tap "Scan"
2. Capture each page
3. Review the scanned pages
4. Tap "Save" when finished

Does the app work offline?

Yes! All scanning and text recognition features work without internet connection.

Which languages are supported?

• English
• Chinese
• Japanese
• Korean
• Devanagari

How do I export my scans?

You can export your scans as:
• PDF documents
• Text files
• Images
